ACT 1

Scene 1

Thunder and Lightning. Enter one Yetch.

YETCH
Hail, trav’ler of these webbèd, wandered paths,
Who, spectral, haunts my clunky typèd code
With cur’ous eyes! Invite I thee: step off
The Ever-On-ing road you read this from.
A moment, leastways, for, perchance, thou’lt find
The bygone form and format I have used
Is pleasing to thine eyes.

In iamb form and Shakespeare’s English send
I you well wishes for the pages ‘head.
And thank I thee, as well, for gen’rous time
Which thou hast lent today.
