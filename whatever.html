This is Bay Brave

Together, we go out on intellectual limbs, test new ideas, develop our unique voices, learn how to take action that makes a positive difference. We are intrepid explorers and innovative thinkers.  

Morning Meeting

We take time to share, listen, and reflect. Youve never met a morning quite like this before.

Immersives

Three weeks, one class, twice a year. Learn by doing. Go deep.  

Mindfulness

Together, we practice being present and centering ourselves for the day ahead.  

Location

Bay is surrounded by the beauty of Presidio National Park and a vibrant, creative community.  

What Will You Create?

Our Senior Signature Projects—SSPs—are the culmination of the Bay experience: Students design and execute a project that addresses a real-world problem and has community impact. The results? Wildly creative, meaningful work.

Nondiscrimination Statement

The Bay School of San Francisco admits students of any sexual orientation or gender identity expression (SOGIE), race, color, religion, ethnicity, national origin, or ancestry to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not discriminate on the basis of SOGIE, race, color, religion, ethnicity, national origin, or ancestry in the administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, flexible tuition programs, athletic programs, and any other school administered programs.
